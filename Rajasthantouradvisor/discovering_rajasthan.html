Know more About Rajasthan

In the hustle and bustle of our daily lives in the busy city of Bangalore, seeking a getaway is a must, and what could be a better destination than the beautiful state of Rajasthan? Apart from being the largest state of Rajasthan, the state has a lot to offer including the rich tapestry of the regal past, enchanting palaces and serene lakes, bustling bazaars, and intricate craftsmanship that can leave any luxurious brand ensembles behind. Rajasthan also referred to as the “Land of Kings” is a treasure trove of history and heritage. The beautiful city of Udaipur surrounded by the lush green hills of Aravlli and serene Lakes is a major tourist attraction attracting tourists from around the globe. Then comes the Pink City, Jaipur, renowned for its trademark building color, the city offers opulent views and beautiful architecture with its gardens, courtyards, museums, and historical significance. Every corner of the enchanting state of Rajasthan offers royalty and a regal past with exotic destinations that you might not have seen ever before that will help you unwind.
